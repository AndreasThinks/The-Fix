The Fix v1.0 - a game for Ludum Dare 25
You're out of cash, and  your last resort is fixing boxing matches.  Throw weapons at the boxers to distract them, and win all the money!

Hit the A,B,G, and P keys to unlock and throw your weapons - all the rest is mostly automatic.  Newspapers interrupt attacks - try to interrupt jumps kicks, they're especially powerful!
The banana lowers your enemies morale, making him use weaker attacks.
